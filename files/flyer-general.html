Free · No app download required · Open to all HI Island businesses
Why Join HIBCW
🔔
Real-Time District Alerts
Your District Captain notifies you the moment significant criminal activity or safety threats occur in your area. Stay ahead of what's happening.
🗺️
Live Incident Map
View every reported incident across Hawaiʻi Island on a live, public map. No login required — open hibcw.kiaahilo.org on any device.
👮
Your District Captain
Each of the 9 districts has a dedicated Captain — your personal contact for local safety issues, incident follow-up, and law enforcement coordination.
🤝
Community Network
Connect with neighboring businesses who share your commitment to safety. HIBCW members look out for each other across the island.
