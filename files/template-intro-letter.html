My name is , and I am the District Captain for HIBCW District — . I'm reaching out because I believe your business would be a valuable part of our community safety network — and I'd love to personally invite you to join.
The Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch (HIBCW) is a free program operated by KIAA that connects local businesses, district captains, and law enforcement across Hawaii Island. Our goal is simple: keep businesses like yours informed and protected through real-time communication, shared awareness, and community support.
As a member, your business receives:
Real-time district alerts — I'll notify you directly when something happens in our area so you can take action quickly.
Verified member badge — Your incident reports on the HIBCW public map display a verified badge, increasing your credibility with the community.
Community network — Connect with neighboring businesses and stay informed about patterns of activity in our district.
Direct line to your captain — You'll have my personal contact information for any safety concerns in our district.
It's completely free — There is no cost to join, and the application takes less than two minutes.
Joining is easy. Simply scan the QR code below or visit hibcw.vercel.app and click "Join HIBCW." Your application comes directly to me and I'll have you approved and connected right away.
