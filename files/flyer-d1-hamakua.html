About HIBCW
The Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch (HIBCW) is a free program operated by KIAA that unites local businesses across Hawaii Island into a coordinated crime awareness and prevention network. As a member, your business gains access to real-time incident alerts, a verified member badge, and direct communication with your District 1 — Hāmākua Captain and the broader HIBCW community.
Member benefits
Real-time district alerts
Receive alerts from your District 1 Captain the moment something happens in your area.
Verified member badge
Your reports on the public HIBCW map display a verified member badge, building credibility.
Community network
Connect with neighboring businesses and your district captain for mutual support and awareness.
Free & easy to join
Membership is completely free. Apply online in under two minutes — no paperwork required.
