Program Overview
Protecting Hawaii Island — One Business at a Time
HIBCW is a free, community-led safety network that connects local businesses, district captains, and law enforcement across all 9 districts of Hawaii Island. Together, we share information, report incidents, and keep our communities safer.
Member Benefits
📢 Real-Time District Alerts
Your District Captain sends alerts the moment something significant happens in your area — suspicious activity, break-ins, known threats — so you're always informed before it escalates.
🗺 Live Incident Map
View all active reported incidents across Hawaii Island in real time at hibcw.vercel.app. No login required — the map is public and always up to date.
👮 Direct Captain Access
Your assigned District Captain is your personal point of contact for all safety concerns. They review reports, coordinate with law enforcement, and keep your district informed.
🤝 Community Network
Connect with neighboring businesses who share your commitment to safety. When one business reports, everyone in the district benefits from that information.
How It Works
1
Witness an incident? Visit hibcw.vercel.app and click "Report an Incident." Fill in details and submit — takes about 2 minutes. No account needed.
2
Your report goes live on the public incident map immediately, visible to HIBCW members and District Captains across the island.
3
Your District Captain reviews the report, coordinates with law enforcement if needed, and sends a district-wide alert if the threat is ongoing.
4
Stay informed — district alerts appear as a banner on the public map in real time. Share the site with your staff so everyone stays in the loop.
