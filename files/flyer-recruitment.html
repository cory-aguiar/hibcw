HIBCW is a free community safety program that connects Hawaiʻi Island businesses with their District Captain, live incident reporting tools, and a network of local businesses committed to keeping the island safe.

How to Join — 4 Easy Steps
1
Scan the QR Code
Point your phone camera at the QR code above or visit hibcw.kiaahilo.org on any device.
2
Complete the Form
Fill in your business name, contact info, district, and a few basic details. Takes about 2 minutes.
3
Get Approved
Your District Captain reviews and approves your application. You'll receive a welcome packet by mail.
4
Stay Connected
Receive district alerts, report incidents, and connect with your HIBCW network.
What You Get as a Member
🔔
District Alerts
Real-time notifications from your Captain when criminal activity or safety threats occur in your area.
🗺️
Incident Map Access
Live map of every reported incident across Hawaiʻi Island, updated in real time. View from any device.
👮
District Captain
A dedicated Captain for your district who coordinates with local law enforcement on your behalf.
📦
Welcome Packet
Receive a printed membership packet including your certificate, district flyer, and quick reference guide.
📋
Registry Listing
Your business is listed in the official HIBCW registry, visible to Captains and partner businesses.
🛡️
Community Safety
Be part of a coordinated network that deters crime, shares intelligence, and protects local businesses.
✅
