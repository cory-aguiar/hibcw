HIBCW Quick Reference
Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch  ·  A Program of KIAA
What to Report
Call 911 + Report to HIBCW: Active crimes, weapons, injuries, suspects on scene
Report to HIBCW: Theft, break-ins, vandalism, fraud, trespassing, harassment
Report to HIBCW: Suspicious persons, vehicles, or activity — even if uncertain
Report to HIBCW: Shoplifting attempts, scam calls/visits targeting your business
Report to HIBCW: Any patterns you notice — same person, same vehicle, repeat incidents
When to Call 911 vs. HIBCW
🔴
Crime in progress, weapon, injury911 FIRST
🟡
Crime already happened, suspect gone911 + HIBCW
🟢
Suspicious activity, no immediate threatHIBCW
🟢
Patterns, tips, community awarenessHIBCW
