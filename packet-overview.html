Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch
A free community safety program connecting businesses, district captains, and law enforcement across Hawaii Island.
Member Benefits
📢 Real-Time District Alerts
Your District Captain sends alerts the moment something significant happens in your area — you're always informed.
🗺 Live Incident Map
View all active reported incidents across Hawaii Island on the public HIBCW map at hibcw.vercel.app.
👮 Direct Captain Access
Your assigned District Captain is your personal point of contact for all safety concerns in your district.
🤝 Community Network
Connect with neighboring businesses who share your commitment to keeping Hawaii Island businesses safe.
How It Works
1
Witness an incident? Visit hibcw.vercel.app and click "Report an Incident." Fill in the details and submit — takes about 2 minutes.
2
Your report goes live on the public incident map immediately, visible to HIBCW members and District Captains across the island.
3
Your District Captain reviews the report, adds follow-up notes, and sends a district alert to other businesses if the threat is ongoing.
4
Stay informed — alerts from your captain appear as a banner on the public map site in real time. No login required to view.
