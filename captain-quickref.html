📢 Alert types & writing guide
⛔ EMERGENCY — imminent threat
👁 BOLO — suspect/vehicle lookout
🟡 AMBER / MISSING — missing person
🚨 INCIDENT — crime in progress / just occurred
🔁 PATTERN — repeat across businesses
💳 SCAM — fraud / counterfeit
🚫 TRESPASS — known trespasser
🫶 WELFARE — concerning person
✅ ALL CLEAR — situation resolved
📋 INFO — general advisory
Every alert should answer:
✓
What — type of incident or threat
✓
Where — street, area, intersection, or business
✓
When — date, time, or "ongoing this week"
✓
What to do — secure valuables, report, avoid area
Critical alerts (EMERGENCY, BOLO, AMBER) auto-display 911 & HPD non-emergency buttons to the public — no need to add them to your message.
Archive, don't delete — archived alerts are kept as a permanent record.
📊 Incident status key
Active
Visible on public map. Investigate, add notes, and archive when resolved. You can restore if archived by mistake.
Archived
Hidden from public map but kept in database. Restore anytime. Cannot permanently delete — contact admin if needed.
Best practice
Always add a follow-up note before archiving. Log HPD contact, resolution, or community outcome for the record.
