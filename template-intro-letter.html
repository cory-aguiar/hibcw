Welcome to Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch
Aloha,
The Hawaii Island Business Crime Watch (HIBCW) is a free community safety program operated by KIAA that connects local businesses, community members, district captains, and law enforcement across all nine districts of Hawaii Island. Our mission is simple: keep Hawaii Island informed and protected through real-time communication, shared awareness, and community cooperation.
HIBCW maintains a public incident map at hibcw.kiaahilo.org/map where anyone can view reported incidents, identify patterns of activity, and stay informed about what's happening in their neighborhood or business district — no account required to view.
